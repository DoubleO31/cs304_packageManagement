The final project is a package manager. Customers can check and organize their order, which contains their packages. They can add order by inputting necessary info, such as package value, delivery type etc. They can also update their order info. Employee will have access to all orders, and is able to check different delivery companies or customers order/ package value. Delivery company will be able to check the orders that are delivered by itself.
